About

A bit about me

I'm Daniel — most people know me online as Heisdanito. I'm a frontend developer currently studying at the University of Education, Winneba (UEW), Ghana, and I spend most of my time building web and mobile products with React, PHP, MySQL and Node.js.

What I enjoy most is taking a rough idea — a school that needs a way to run elections online, a restaurant that needs an ordering system — and turning it into something people can actually open and use. I care about the small details: layout, spacing, and how a page feels to click through, not just whether it works.

Outside of development, I also do graphic design and video editing, so I tend to think about a project's visuals as much as its code.
